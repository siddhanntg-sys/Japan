Famous sites

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is the highest mountain in Japan, standing at 3,776 meters (12,389 feet). It is an iconic symbol of Japan and is known for its symmetrical cone shape. Mount Fuji is a popular destination for climbers and tourists alike, offering breathtaking views from its summit.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a historic castle located in Osaka, Japan. It is one of the most famous castles in Japan and is known for its impressive architecture and rich history. The castle has been rebuilt several times throughout the years and now stands as a symbol of Osaka.

Okinawa

Okinawa is an island prefecture in Japan known for its unique culture, beautiful beaches, and rich history. It is a popular destination for tourists seeking a tropical getaway with a distinct Japanese flavor.
